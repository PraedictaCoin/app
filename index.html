❓ FAQ

🔮 What is a Praediction?
A praediction is a statement about a future event. Users stake PRAE on YES/NO. Oracles vote after the deadline, and if 66% consensus is reached, the winning side gets rewards.
🦉 Who are the oracles?
Any PRAE holder can vote. Voting power = balance × (0.5 + reputation). Reputation increases with correct votes.
⚖️ How do disputes work?
After a praediction resolves, anyone can dispute within 7 days by posting a bond (500 PRAE) and evidence. The admin then decides – if the dispute is accepted, the outcome flips and the bond is returned; if rejected, the bond is slashed.
💰 Do I lose my 777 PRAE?
No – the app only checks your balance. Your tokens stay in your wallet.
📈 How does auto‑resolve work?
If you choose a price feed and a target value, the system will automatically resolve the market after the deadline based on the real price.
