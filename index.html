❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔮 What is a Praediction?
A praediction is a statement about a future event. Users simply choose YES or NO. Oracles vote after the deadline, and if 66% consensus is reached, the winning side earns Seerscore points.
🦉 Who are the oracles?
Any PRAE holder can vote. Voting power = balance × (0.5 + reputation). Reputation increases with correct votes.
⚖️ How do disputes work?
After a praediction resolves, anyone can dispute within 7 days by posting 7 voting rights and evidence. The oracle then decides – if the dispute is accepted, the outcome flips and the bond is returned; if rejected, the bond is slashed.
💎 Do I lose my 777 PRAE?
No – the app only checks your balance. Your tokens stay in your wallet. No money is ever transferred.
📈 How does auto‑resolve work?
If you select a price feed (e.g., CoinGecko BTC) and a target value, after the deadline the system automatically fetches the real price. If the price ≥ target, outcome YES; else NO. Supported coins: BTC, ETH, SOL, DOGE, MATIC, AVAX, LINK.

PRAE is a community token used for gatekeeping against bots and scammers. You can get it on DexScreener.
