❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🔮 What is a Praediction?
A praediction is a statement about a future event. You can prove than you are a Seer and climb the ranks of the Board to the top.
🦉 Who are the oracles?
Any PRAE holder can vote. Voting power = balance × (0.5 + reputation). Reputation increases with correct votes.
⚖️ How do disputes work?
After a praediction resolves, anyone can dispute within 7 days by posting a bond (500 PRAE) and evidence. The Oracle then decides – if the dispute is accepted, the outcome flips and the bond is returned; if rejected, the bond is slashed (half to admin).
💰 Do I lose my 777 PRAE?
No – the app only checks your balance. Your tokens stay in your wallet.
🎲 What can I praedict?
Anything with a verifiable source (URL). Crypto, sports, elections, or wildcard.
🏆 How are points calculated?
Base points: 100‑200 depending on category + bonuses for early prediction, long term, underdog, and correct streaks.
📱 Can I install this as an app?
Yes – on mobile Chrome, click “Add to Home Screen”. Works offline as PWA.
